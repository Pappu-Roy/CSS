Bangladesh (officially the People's Republic of Bangladesh) is a South Asian country known for its rich history, dense population, and unique geography. Here is a summary of key information about Bangladesh: Key Facts Capital and Largest City: Dhaka Official Language: Bengali (Bangla) Population: Over 170 million (eighth-most populous country in the world) Area: Approximately 148,460 square kilometers (57,320 sq mi) Government: Unitary parliamentary republic Currency: Bangladeshi Taka (BDT) National Anthem: "Amar Shonar Bangla" (My Golden Bengal), written by Rabindranath Tagore National Day: March 26 (Independence Day) Borders: Shares land borders with India on three sides (north, west, and east) and Myanmar to the southeast. It has a coastline on the Bay of Bengal to the south.

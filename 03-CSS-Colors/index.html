Bangladesh, officially the People's Republic of Bangladesh, is a country in South Asia. Known for its rich cultural heritage and lush, riverine landscape, it has a history marked by ancient civilizations, colonial rule, and a hard-won independence.

The history of the region dates back millennia, with a succession of Buddhist, Hindu, and Muslim dynasties. It became a hub of trade and culture under the Mughal Empire. The British East India Company gained control of the region in the 18th century, and it remained under British rule until 1947.
